Shih Tzu

Shih Tzuz are small dogs with short snouts and a long coat of fur. They have the breed standard underbite to give them their special Shih Tzu look. The breed is beleived to come from China; its name translates to lion since the breed was used to represent the lion in oriental art.

Bichon Frise

Here is a picure of a, most likley, full grown Bichon Frise. Bichon means small long-haired dog from French, which is exactly what it is. They are usualy white small long haired dogs with a tremendoud amount of energy.

Chow Chow

Chow Chow dogs are a breed that has come from Northern China. Their name translates into puffy lion dog, which perfectly discries the dog. The breed actually created the teddy bear since, long story short, Queen Victoria had a stuffed one made by the mannerism of her puppy.

Maltese

The Maltese dog was originally nicknamed "The Ancient Dog of Malta" with it's original unknown, many beleive it oringinated form Asia. It is a small white long-haired dog like the bichon but has a slightly different nose shape.

Cockapoo Puppy

Cockapoos, otherwise know as spoodle are a mix between the Cocker Spaniel and a Poodle. They are very livley loving dogs with short hair and minmal shedding and dandruff.

Maltipoo Puppy

Maltipoos are small dogs that can come in a variety of colors. The are always biocolored which means they have a mix between two or more colors on their body. They are playful energetic dogs who need a lot of attention.
